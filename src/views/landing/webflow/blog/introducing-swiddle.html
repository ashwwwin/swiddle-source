Introducing Swiddle

Introduction
When I was younger, I spent hours daily on Club Penguin & made some of my closest friends on it. I still remember the excitement to get home from school so I could get online.

Socializing over the internet feels broken, we have a stage to talk to the masses but, the way we communicate in smaller groups has stayed the same for the past 10 years since Snapchat. We can do better. Club Penguin was a hint into the future of what socializing over the internet could be.

We wrote the first line of code for what would become Swiddle [1] on November 27, 2020. There's a revolution happening right in front of us, and if it weren't for a chance encounter I am unsure that we'd be a part of it [2]. We weren't thinking of Web3 when we started but, we're turning the ship towards the right direction.

The work we've done over the past year has primed us for this moment. We could not be in a better position to bring the Metaverse to you. There are many Metaverse projects but, we are amongst the few to ever mint NFTs after having a live product and the first to pivot from Web2 to Web3 as a metaverse.

We've been doing this since the Metaverse was referred to as a MMORPG and proven that we can execute. I haven't spent time building a following on Twitter and aware that scams are abundant in the crypto space so I encourage you to join our Discord, ask questions, join our weekly calls and be a part of our community.
