Introducing Swiddle

Introduction
Socializing over the internet feels broken, we have a stage to talk to the masses but, the way we communicate with each other has stayed the same for the past 10 years. Club Penguin and other games from the Flash era were a hint into the future of what socializing over the internet could be.

When I was younger, I spent hours daily on Club Penguin & made some of my closest friends on it. I still remember the being 8 and feeling the excitement to get home from school so I could get online. Soon after, when I was 13 - I made my first virtual world on Flash.

7 years later, we wrote the first line of code for what would become Swiddle [1] on November 27, 2020. The work we've done over the past year has primed us for this moment. There are many Metaverse projects but, we are the first to pivot from Web2 to Web3 as a Metaverse and amongst the few to mint NFTs with a live beta.

We've been doing this since the Metaverse was referred to as a MMORPG and proven that we can execute. I haven't spent time building a following on Twitter and aware that scams are abundant in the crypto space however, I encourage you to join our Discord, join our weekly calls and check out our beta. We are here for the long haul.
