Introducing Swiddle

Introduction
Socializing over the internet feels broken, we have a stage to talk to the masses but, the way we communicate with each other has stayed the same for the past 10 years. Club Penguin and other games from the Flash era were a hint into the future of what socializing over the internet could be.

Growing up, I spent hours daily on Club Penguin & made some of my closest friends on it. I still remember the being 8 and feeling the excitement to get home from school so I could get online. Soon after, when I was 13 - I made my first virtual world on Flash.

7 years later, we wrote the first line of code for what would become Swiddle [1] on November 27, 2020. The work we've done over the past year has primed us for this moment. We've been doing this since the Metaverse was referred to as a MMORPG.
[1] Swiddle is a rebrand from our previous name, Only Friends. We loved the name but, we found ourselves having to explain the funny part too often.
