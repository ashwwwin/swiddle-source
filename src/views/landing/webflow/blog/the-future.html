The Future

Places, items, games all created by the people for the people. Everything built on top of another. The value created for each other far surpasses the amount created for the institution. The foundation that we build together could outlast our lifetime.

The fine balance between a great product and great crypto foundation is where the future lives. This blend of artistry and craftsmanship will unleash great things into the world. It's the revolution for the makers.
‍
We can see the widespread adoption amongst the tech community but, what about everyone else? If you're in a tech community, you probably feel like everyone is into crypto but, that's just about 8 million people - the world has 8 billion.

The magnetic pull of Web3 is unstoppable and it's slowly spreading towards the mainstream. It's just a matter of time. All that we're trying to do is make the spread happen sooner.
‍
Not every generation gets to witness the remaking of financial systems and the rules of ownership. The future could not be more exciting. What a time to be alive.
