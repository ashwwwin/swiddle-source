The Future

Places, items, games all created by the people for the people. Everything built on top of another. The value created for each other far surpasses the amount that could ever be created for an institution. The future is decentralized and the foundation that we build together could last generations.

Not every generation gets to witness the remaking of financial systems, the rules of ownership and what freedom really feels like. The future could not be more exciting. If you're in a tech community, you probably feel like everyone is into crypto. That's just 8 million people - the world has 8 billion. The question is not if but when will it go mainstream, all we're trying to do is speed up that timeline.
‍
A bad version of the future is one where an institution owns the Metaverse. Our end game is to die as an institution and to be reborn as the foundation of a decentralized Metaverse running entirely on a p2p network. I believe that there will be one giant p2p network with different 'portals'.

These portals would somewhat act as governments, everyone free to choose their own rule system or none. eg. there could be a portal where trolling is welcome - and another where trolling is banned, all while running on the same network. The person could never get banned from the network. This is something that is not possible when it is owned by an institution due to eg. political influences. It has to be truly free in order to be The Metaverse.

Swiddle is an experimental portal at it's earliest stages, what we learn & discover will go in to building the decentralized foundation of The Metaverse. If we succeed and are right about the future, the work that we do will last lifetimes, what will happen in the next 10 years will set the foundation for the next 200. I believe that this time will go down as a defining moment in history and we need to not fuck up the foundation.
